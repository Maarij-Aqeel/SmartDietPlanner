Mediterranean Grilled Salmon

Last Updated: December 8, 2024
Ratings: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
Tags
Keto
Gluten-free
High-protein

Description

A simple yet flavorful salmon recipe infused with Mediterranean spices, perfect for a healthy dinner or meal prep.

Ingredients

For Dish
  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
For Garnish:
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced into wedges

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade:

    In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, smoked paprika, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper.

  2. Marinate the Salmon:

    Brush the marinade generously over the salmon fillets and let them sit for 15-20 minutes.

  3. Preheat the Grill:

    Heat your grill (or grill pan) to medium-high and lightly oil the grates.

  4. Grill the Salmon:

    Place the salmon fillets skin-side down on the grill. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (62°C).

  5. Prepare the Garnish:

    In a small bowl, mix cherry tomatoes, olives, parsley, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

  6. Serve and Enjoy!

    Plate the grilled salmon and top it with the garnish. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.
